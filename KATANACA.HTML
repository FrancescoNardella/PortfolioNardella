About Project

Project that lasted 3 days, which consists in identifying errors present in an elaborate, and then correct them. There was a division into groups where everyone had their own task. To realize this project it was necessary for our group to use 2 software: Photoshop used to graphically correct errors, and Powerpoint to represent the work made on Photoshop. I learned to discuss and find every detail, I deepened my skills on Photoshop and Powerpoint and above all I managed to reinforcemy quality in working in a team.
