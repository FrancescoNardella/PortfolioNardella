About Energy Way

This company was born as a Start Up in November 2013. Over the years it has grown into an innovative PMI of mathematicians, engineers, physicists and neuroscientists, experts in Data Science applied to sectors such as: Energy, Ceramics, Utility, Retail (GDO), Finance and many others. Today over 200 companies in the area have chosen their services and mathematical models for the optimization of business processes. They collaborate with multinationals such as Google and IBM, with which they have access to specialized libraries, for subjects with an advanced development profile.

About Project

Project lasted 3 days, where we worked in a group. The aim was to create a data collection based on the Reggio Emilia site and then think of a Start-up idea. Our idea was to collect data on the amount of books bought in Reggio Emilia. The result of the collection was to have a high percentage of sales of history and geography books. With these data we have developed an idea of a Start-up, which has the function of telling stories of the Reggio area, so as to enhance it. I learned to collect data, so that I can analyze and evaluate marketing ideas for a future Start-up.
